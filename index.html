About Lola Lydia

A Life Well Lived

Lola Lydia has been a pillar of strength, love, and wisdom for our family for 90 incredible years. Her kindness, resilience, and joyful spirit have touched countless lives.

Born in 1933, she has witnessed and adapted to tremendous changes in the world while maintaining her core values of faith, family, and compassion.
